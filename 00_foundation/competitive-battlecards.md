# Competitive Battlecards

## Positioning frame

TheHog.ai competes against static B2B data providers, enrichment workflow tools, GTM intelligence products, and AI command-center narratives. The strongest wedge is unified, real-time, agent-ready intelligence in one API and command center.

## Clay

### Clay customer view

Clay is the flexible enrichment workflow builder for power users.

### Clay wedge

TheHog.ai reduces provider stitching and adds real-time social and market signals as a first-class layer.

### Clay displacement angle

Use TheHog.ai when the workflow starts with live market context and agent consumption, not only enrichment waterfall orchestration.

## Apollo

### Apollo customer view

Apollo is contact data plus sales engagement for broad outbound teams.

### Apollo wedge

TheHog.ai starts account-first and adds live signals and structured research before contact activation.

### Apollo displacement angle

Use TheHog.ai when static contact search is not enough to identify why now.

## ZoomInfo

### ZoomInfo customer view

ZoomInfo is the enterprise B2B data and GTM platform incumbent.

### ZoomInfo wedge

TheHog.ai is lighter, API-native, and agent-first for teams building their own GTM systems.

### ZoomInfo displacement angle

Use TheHog.ai when speed, agent workflows, and unified structured context matter more than enterprise suite breadth.

## Keyplay / PeerSignal / HG Insights

### Signal tools customer view

Signal and account intelligence tools for category tracking and TAM building.

### Signal tools wedge

TheHog.ai combines signal capture with people discovery, enrichment, deep research, and MCP-native agent workflows.

### Signal tools displacement angle

Use TheHog.ai when signals need to become automated GTM actions inside the same data layer.
