# RevOps Head ICP

## Profile

Revenue operations leader responsible for data quality, GTM systems, routing, scoring, attribution, and pipeline efficiency.

## Jobs to be done

- Reduce data staleness.
- Standardize enrichment and signal inputs.
- Improve ICP scoring from firmographic and behavioral signals.
- Lower operational overhead from multi-tool GTM stacks.

## Friction

- Needs cost predictability.
- Needs CRM integration patterns.
- Needs governance around signals, dedupe, and enrichment quality.

## Buying trigger

The current GTM stack produces stale, expensive, or hard-to-operationalize data.
