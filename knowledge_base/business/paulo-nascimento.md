# Paulo Nascimento

Co-founder and CTO of [[thehog-ai-company]]. The technical architect behind the Hog's API, enrichment pipeline, and agent-native infrastructure.

## Background

**B.S. in Computer Science, University of Washington.** Self-describes as a "passionate entrepreneur & engineer who loves building products at scale."

AI specialist who started winning hackathons at age 14. Career trajectory shows a pattern of being the earliest technical hire at high-stakes companies:

- **Auth0** — Youngest hire in company history (age 17). Later served as the company's first GenAI engineer under Okta, architecting tools that accelerated developer onboarding.
- **Ravenna** (backed by Khosla & Madrona) — First AI hire. Built agentic capabilities for IT workflows.
- **Patent pending** in multi-agent management — a specialist in autonomous systems.

LinkedIn bio: "Using leadership, storytelling, and software engineering, I create and grow products by providing a clear vision and perspective to teams and individuals."

## Role at TheHog.ai

- **Title:** Co-founder & CTO (Sep 2025 – present)

- **Owns:** [[thehog-ai-api]] design, [[hog-api-enrichment]] pipeline, [[mcp-integration]] protocol, multi-provider waterfall routing
