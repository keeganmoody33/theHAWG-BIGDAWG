# Hudson Liao

CEO and Co-founder of [[thehog-ai-company]]. The "Head of Growth" the product is named after — the dream tool he wished he had during his operator years. San Francisco native.

## Background

Nearly 15 years in GTM with a track record as a 5× first growth and marketing hire at startups. Two standout results define his operating thesis:

- Took a startup from ~$900k to $7M+ ARR in 3 months as a solo growth hire
- Pivoted another company to profitability with only two months of runway left

Built repeatable playbooks across search, social, outbound, partnerships, and product-led motions. This operator experience is the source material behind [[gtm-engineering]] and the [[icp-segmentation]] framework — he lived the pain of stitching together fragmented data from a dozen providers before building the unified layer.

### Beyond Tech

Founded **Asians Are Strong** (March 17, 2021 — one day after the Atlanta spa shootings). Mission: protect and empower the Asian community. This community-building instinct carries into how he thinks about GTM — growth through genuine connection, not spray-and-pray.

## Role at TheHog.ai

- **Title:** CEO, Co-founder (Sep 2025 – present)
- **Function:** GTM strategy, product vision, growth operations
- **Owns:** Product direction, go-to-market execution, customer development
- **Operating style:** "The GTM person you call when outcomes matter"
