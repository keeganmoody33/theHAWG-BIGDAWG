# ICP: Solo Founder / Early-Stage Operator

Solo founders building GTM from zero — they wear every hat (product, engineering, sales, marketing) and need tools that collapse complexity rather than adding it. They cannot afford dedicated RevOps or GTM engineering hires, so they need the [[thehog-ai-company]] platform to act as their GTM engineering layer.

This segment has the highest urgency (they're spending time, not money, on prospecting) but the lowest budget. The value prop is time-to-pipeline: how fast can they go from "I need customers" to "I have qualified conversations."

## Key Points

- **Pain:** Manual prospecting eats founder time; every hour spent researching leads is an hour not building product
- **Decision criteria:** Speed of setup, cost transparency, no-code-required onboarding
- **Buying signal:** Using LinkedIn Sales Nav manually, building spreadsheet-based pipelines
- **Disqualifier:** Pre-revenue with no clear ICP hypothesis yet
- **Messaging angle:** "Your first 50 qualified leads in 30 minutes, not 30 hours"
