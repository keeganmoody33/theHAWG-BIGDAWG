# GTM Engineer ICP

## Profile

Technical GTM operator at a B2B SaaS or DevTools company. Comfortable with APIs, enrichment pipelines, CSV exports, CRMs, and automation tools.

## Jobs to be done

- Build company-first TAM lists.
- Discover people scoped to selected accounts.
- Enrich contacts with verified emails and phones.
- Feed structured account intelligence into outbound and CRM workflows.
- Replace or compress Clay and Apollo waterfall steps where real-time signal coverage matters.

## Friction

- Needs batch/concurrency guidance for enrichment.
- Needs rate-limit and cache freshness details.
- Needs clear semantics for `match_score`, `growth_band`, `tech_stack`, and `signal_summary`.

## Buying trigger

A team needs an agent-ready real-time data layer instead of stitched enrichment providers.
