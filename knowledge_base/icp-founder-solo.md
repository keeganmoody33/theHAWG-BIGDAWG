# Solo Founder ICP

## Profile

Founder-led startup with little or no GTM team. May be non-technical or lightly technical. Needs practical execution more than API detail.

## Jobs to be done

- Find the right companies.
- Identify reachable buyers.
- Understand where prospects talk online.
- Draft first outreach or social engagement.

## Friction

- Confusion between command center UI and developer API.
- Async polling feels broken when not explained.
- Credit consumption needs clearer product copy.

## Buying trigger

A founder needs to turn market research into outbound without hiring a GTM team.
